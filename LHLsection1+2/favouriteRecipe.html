Ingredients

  • Frozen tilapia filets
  • Butter
  • Lemon juice
  • Parsley flakes
  • Salt & pepper

Directions

  1. Place filets in alumnium foil
  2. Spread butter on filets (1 tbsp/filet)
  3. Add lemon juice to taste
  4. Add parsley, salt, and pepper to taste
  5. Cover and bake at 450℉ for 30 minutes
